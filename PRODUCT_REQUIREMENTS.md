# Momentry PRD

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Owner: Yadhu

---

# Vision

Momentry is a private digital storybook where couples preserve their relationship through beautifully organized chapters and moments.

It is not a social network.

It is not cloud storage.

It is not another gallery app.

Momentry exists to make people relive the moments that matter.

---

# Mission

Help couples remember the little moments before they become forgotten memories.

---

# Core Values

- Story over timeline
- Emotion over engagement
- Privacy over virality
- Simplicity over features
- Quality over speed

---

# Target Users

Primary:
Couples (18–35)

Secondary:
Families
Friends
Travel partners

---

# MVP Goals

A user can:

- Create an account
- Create one Story
- Invite one Partner
- Create Chapters
- Add Moments
- View their Story
- Edit Moments

---

# Success Metric

A couple successfully creates their story and adds their first moment within 10 minutes.

---

# Non Goals

No public feed

No likes

No followers

No chat

No AI

No notifications

No analytics

---

# North Star

Someone opens Momentry after ten years and smiles.
