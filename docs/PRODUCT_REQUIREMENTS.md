# Momentry - Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Status: Draft
Owner: Yadhu

---

# Vision

Momentry is a private digital storybook where two people preserve the story of their relationship through chapters and moments.

It is designed to make people relive memories, not just store them.

---

# Mission

Help couples remember the little moments before they become forgotten memories.

---

# Core Principles

- Story over timeline
- Emotion over engagement
- Privacy over virality
- Simplicity over complexity
- Quality over quantity

---

# Target Audience

Primary:
- Couples (18–35)

Future Expansion:
- Families
- Friends
- Personal journals
- Travel journals

---

# MVP Features

Authentication
- Sign Up
- Sign In
- Logout

Story
- Create Story
- Edit Story
- Story Cover

Partner
- Invite Partner
- Accept Invitation

Chapters
- Create Chapter
- Edit Chapter
- Delete Chapter

Moments
- Add Moment
- Edit Moment
- Delete Moment
- Image Upload

Gallery
- View Moments
- Filter by Chapter

---

# Success Metric

A new couple should be able to:

- Create an account
- Create a story
- Invite a partner
- Add their first moment

within 10 minutes.

---

# Non Goals

- Social feed
- Likes
- Followers
- Public profiles
- Chat
- AI-generated captions
- Ads

---

# North Star

Someone opens Momentry years later and smiles while reliving their story.
